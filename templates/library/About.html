About Us

Waterstones is Glasgow's first e-library that enables the students of Glasgow to read and educate themselves free of charge This is an attempt to motivate students to constantly read without worrying about a pric to pay. Without a physical store, students reserve a book that intrigues them and we will ship it to them within 1-2 business days. Students are able to reserve a book for 30 days, and will be asked to return the book
